Craving some delicious Indian food? Maybe you're in the mood for a juicy goat curry? No matter what kind of meal you have in mind, Lars' Restaurant is ready to prepare it for you. Since the early 2000s, Lars' Restaurant has been the go-to diner for residents of Dallas, TX. Our Restaurant serves wholesome and flavorful dining options for lunch and dinner. From curry to rice, seafood to meats, you'll find all kinds of hearty meals prepared fresh at Lars' Restaurant. Our resutrant also has a full bakery with delicious baked goods and other treats, including our famous cheesecake. Sounds delicious, right? We're here in Dallas, Texas to serve you the best Indian food around, whenever you're looking for a great Indian restaurant in Dallas, TX.
